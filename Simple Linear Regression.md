# Coefficients 
$$
\hat{\beta_{0}} = \bar{y} - \hat{\beta}_{1}\bar{x}
$$
$$
\hat{\beta_{1}} = \hat{\rho}_{xy} \cdot \left( \frac{\hat{\sigma}_{y}}{\hat{\sigma}_{x}} \right)= \frac{{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}(x_{i}- \bar{x})(y_{i}-\bar{y})}}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}(x_{i}-\bar{x})^{2}}
$$
