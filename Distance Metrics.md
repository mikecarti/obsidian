### Cosine
![[Pasted image 20230726132433.png | 500]]

$$
\text{cosine similarity} := \cos(\theta) = \frac{A*B}{\|A\|\|B\|}= \frac{\sum_{n=1}^{n}{A_iB_i}}{\sqrt{\sum_{n=1}^{n}A^2_{1} * \sum_{n=1}^{n}B^2_{i}}}
$$
$A, B - \text{are vectors}$
$A_{i}, B_{i} - \text{vector elements}$
