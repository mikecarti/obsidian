## Definitions
$$
\begin{align}
&\sigma^{2} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_{i}-\bar{x})^{2} \\
&\sigma^{2} = cov(X, X) \\
&\sigma^{2} = \mathbb{E}[X^{2}] - \mathbb{E}[X]^{2}
\end{align}
$$
## Properties
$$
\begin{align}
&Var(aX+b) = a^{2}Var(X) \\
&Var (X-Y) = Var(X)+Var(Y)−2Cov(X,Y) \\
&Var(−Y)=Var(Y)
\end{align}
$$
