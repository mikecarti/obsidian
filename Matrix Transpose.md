$$
(n \times m)^{T} = (m\times n)
$$

## Interesting Properties
1) $(A+B)^{T}= A^{T}+ B^{T}$
2) $(ABC)^{T}=C^{T}B^{T}A^{T}$
3) $(cA)^{T}=cA^{T}$
4) $\det(A^{T})=\det(A)$
5) $(A^{T})^{-1}=(A^{-1})^{T}$
6) If $A$ is a square matrix, its [[Eigenvalue|eigenvalues]] are equal to eigenvalues of its transpose.
