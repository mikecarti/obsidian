### Global models
- high bias, low variance
- Assumes many of unrealistic assumptions:
	- additive error
	- Single error source
	- Linear regression and linearity

### Local models
- e.g. [[K Nearest Neighbors (KNN)]]
- low bias, high variance
- Few assumptions
