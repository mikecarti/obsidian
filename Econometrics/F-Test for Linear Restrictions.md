## Why we use [[Fischer Distribution]]?
$$
F = \frac{{(RSS_{R}-RSS_{UR})}/q}{RSS_{UR}/(n-k)} \sim \frac{\chi_{q}^{2} - \chi^{2}_{q}}{\chi^{2}_{n-k}} \equiv {\frac{\chi^{2}_{q}}{\chi^{2}_{n-k}}} := \text{Fischer}
$$
