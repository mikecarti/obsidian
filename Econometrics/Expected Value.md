$$
### Properties
$$
\begin{align}
&\mathbf{E}[aX] = a\mathbf{E}[X] \\
&\mathbf{E}[X+Y] = \mathbf{E}[X] + \mathbf{E}[Y] \\
&\mathbf{E}[XY] = \mathbf{E}[X]\mathbf{E}[Y] \quad \text{for independent } X, Y
\end{align}
$$
