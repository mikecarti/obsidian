Partial Corelation lets us fix some variables (giving us a correlation ceteris parabus)

n
# Definition
$$
pCorr(X,Y;Z) = Corr(X^{*}, Y^{*})
$$

given $X^{*} = X-\alpha Z$,  $Y^{*} = Y - \alpha Z$
$Cov(X^{*}, Z)= 0$          $Cov(Y^{*}, Z)=0$
